Why is 1917 my favourite film?

Interesting cinematography and believable story, about two ordinary soldiers tasked with saving more than a thousand people by delivering a single message.

My favourite film 1917

The film is about two British soldiers that are tasked with delivering a message to another battalion, warning them of walking into a trap where more than a thousand soldiers could die. What immediately stands out about the film is that it's made in one continual shot, right until the end.

Furthermore it does a great job at retaining suspense as it is shown the protagonists are no heroes: They are simply normal people that are tasked with a mission of immense importance.

This makes the story very relatable and believable
